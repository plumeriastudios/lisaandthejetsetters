Travel Tips & Tricks

Pack Smart

Bring reef-safe sunscreen, a reusable water bottle, and light layers. Pack a small day bag for excursions and always keep copies of important documents.

Stay Connected

Download offline maps before you go. Share your itinerary with family. Learn a few local phrases to connect with residents and enhance your experience.

Embrace Local Culture

Try street food, visit local markets, and talk to residents. The best travel memories come from authentic experiences and unexpected conversations.

Eat Like a Local

Skip touristy restaurants and follow locals to food stalls and family-run eateries. You'll save money, eat better food, and discover authentic flavors you won't find in guidebooks.

Book Smart, Not Just Cheap

Travel during shoulder season for lower prices and fewer crowds. Book accommodation slightly outside main tourist zones. Use local transportation instead of taxis to save significantly.

Free Activities First

Beaches, hiking trails, local festivals, and sunset watching are free. Many museums have free entry days. Walking tours often work on tips, letting you pay what you can afford.
